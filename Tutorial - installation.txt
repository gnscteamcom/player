
Installation

1. upload and extract proxy 1.7.1.zip and extract in public_html/proxylocated
2. Create Mysql database and user database
2. upload db.sql in phpmyadmin in your new database
3. Replace URL in phpmyadmin - Settings - https://demo.pecintadrama.com by its domain, where the script is located:

Example: https://www.yourdomain.com/proxylocaded

3. Config user, database and other dates in proxy\config.php

4. Go to Login:

https://www.yourdomain.com/proxylocaded/admin

User : demo@demo.com
Pass : 12345678

Modify pass in manage users

How to change SKID AD (VAST) video ads:

1. Open in your host...script\templates\jwplayer\template.php
2. Change Line 34 or/or Line 39, please enter the URL XML Tag Your VAST Ad

Definition of displacement in ad settings:

pre: the ad appears before the video is played
post: the ad appears after the video is finished
50%: the ad appears in the middle of the video.

OR Delete VAST ADS.
